Shop Elasticity Analysis

Highlights

  • Ran log-log demand model to quantify true price sensitivity, delivering a direct %ΔQuantity per %ΔPrice while controlling for promotions, seasonality, and shop-specific factors
  • Built shop-level elasticity metrics - Delta Daily QTY (the change in daily quantity sold by price elasticity)for and Price Elasticity,to capture localized pricing power
  • Applied K-means clustering on the two metrics to segment shops into high-, medium-, and low-elasticity clusters, enabling targeted pricing by clusters
